// FEATURED REVIEW · DRAMA
Interstellar
Christopher Nolan · 2014 · 169 min
"I watched this at 1am and cried for reasons I still can't explain. Something about time, love, and the universe hit me like a freight train."

Nolan doesn't make movies — he builds entire worlds and dares you to feel something while thinking too hard. Interstellar is the film that made me realise how small all our problems are, and somehow that made me feel better about everything. The docking scene alone deserves an essay.
